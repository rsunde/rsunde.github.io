Debugging 'Glitch' the Caterpillar

Testing Ground

This page mimics the Hacker theme layout to test the caterpillar animation.

Control Panel

What to test:

  • Arrival: Reload the page. Does Glitch drop from the top or crawl from the side?
  • Crawling: Does Glitch crawl along the green dotted line (bottom of header)?
  • Interaction: Move your mouse close to Glitch. Does he get scared (turn red/run)?
  • Responsiveness: Resize the window. If Glitch is cut off, does he respawn?
